MemoryCache is an experimental development project to turn a local desktop environment into a personal on-device AI agent.

What's included?

Locally-hosted privateGPT instance

Browser-based interface for working with your insights

Firefox extension for adding to your cache from the browser

Llamafile LLM options for finding the right model for your usecase
